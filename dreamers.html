Tapa Amanya Dreamers Association

“Supporting one another in times of need and joy”

Who We Are

The Tapa Amanya Dreamers Association is built on unity, compassion, and shared dreams. We support our members financially and socially during important moments such as funerals, outdooring ceremonies, weddings, graduations, and more. Beyond these events, we aim to inspire young people, foster growth, and bring hope to every family in our town. Together, we dream bigger and build stronger.

Our Activities

Funeral Support

We stand by members in difficult times, providing financial and emotional support during funerals.

Outdooring Ceremonies

Together we celebrate new life, welcoming newborns with love and blessings.

Graduation Support

Encouraging education by supporting members during graduations and academic achievements.

Community Projects

Beyond personal support, we join hands to improve our town through clean-ups, donations, and initiatives.

Regular Meetings

Our members meet regularly to plan, encourage, and ensure that no one is left behind.
